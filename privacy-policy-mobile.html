Mobile App Privacy Policy - Laine

Last updated: April 2026

App: Laine (Android & iOS)

Audio Recording Permission

Our app requires microphone access for:

  • Voice-to-text message input
  • Live talk features with AI companion
  • Voice commands and interactions

Audio Data Processing

  • Audio is processed in real-time for speech-to-text conversion
  • Voice recordings are not permanently stored
  • Processed text is used for AI response generation
  • All transmission uses end-to-end encryption

Other Permissions

  • Network access: For AI communication
  • Notifications: For reminders and messages
  • Storage: For local app data and preferences

Data Sharing

  • Audio data shared only with AI service providers
  • No data sold to third parties
  • No marketing use of personal data

User Control

  • Revoke microphone permission anytime in device settings
  • Delete conversation history in app settings
  • Delete account and all associated data
