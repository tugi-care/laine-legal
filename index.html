Privacy Policy for Tugi Companion

Last updated: April 2026

Audio Data Collection

Our Laine app records audio when you use the voice input feature to send messages to the AI companion. This includes:

  • Voice recordings for speech-to-text conversion
  • Audio data processed for AI response generation

How We Use Audio Data

  • Convert speech to text for message sending
  • Provide conversational AI responses
  • Improve voice recognition accuracy

Data Storage & Security

  • Audio data is processed securely and not stored permanently
  • Voice recordings are deleted after processing
  • All communication uses encrypted connections (HTTPS)

Data Sharing

  • Audio data is only shared with our AI service providers for processing
  • We do not sell or share your audio data with third parties for marketing

Your Rights

  • You can review and delete your conversation history
  • You can revoke microphone access in device settings
  • You can request data deletion under privacy regulations
